Lasgana

Description

We gotta lotta meat in some sauce. There's some cheese and these big flat noodles.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Brown the meat
  2. Make the sauce
  3. Boil the noodles
  4. Layer the meat, sauce, cheese, and noodles
  5. Bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes
